# Career Opportunities in IT Security

## What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity protects systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access or attacks.

## Red Team vs Blue Team

### Red Team
Simulates attacks to identify weaknesses.

### Blue Team
Defends systems and monitors threats.

## Career Opportunities

- Security Analyst
- Penetration Tester
- Vulnerability Assessor
- Threat Hunter
- DFIR Analyst
- Malware Analyst
- Security Researcher

## Security Operations Center (SOC)

SOC responsibilities:
- Monitor security events
- Detect threats
- Respond to incidents

## Three Pillars of Cybersecurity

### People
Training and awareness.

### Process
Policies and procedures.

### Technology
Security tools and systems.

## Vulnerability Scanning

Automated identification of security weaknesses.

## Penetration Testing

Simulated attacks to validate vulnerabilities.

### Penetration Testing Lifecycle
1. Reconnaissance
2. Enumeration
3. Gaining Access
4. Maintaining Access
5. Covering Tracks
6. Reporting
